About Us

Learn more about KIRA Clinic and our mission

Who We Are

KIRA Clinic is a private health center located in Nyagatare District, Eastern Province of Rwanda. Established with a vision to provide accessible and quality healthcare to the community, we have grown to become a trusted medical facility in the region.

Our Mission

To deliver compassionate, high-quality, and affordable healthcare services to every patient who walks through our doors. We strive to improve the health and well-being of our community through excellent medical care and health education.

Our Vision

To be the leading healthcare provider in Nyagatare District, known for our commitment to patient care, medical excellence, and community service.

Our Values

  • Compassion: We treat every patient with kindness and respect
  • Excellence: We strive for the highest quality in all we do
  • Integrity: We are honest and ethical in our practices
  • Accessibility: We make healthcare available to all
